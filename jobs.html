Cloud Engineer(infrastucture and Automation)

Position overview

Ref: Idk â€” Title: Cloud Engineer

Brief: Design, build and operate scalable cloud infrastructure and automation pipelines to support production services and developer platforms.

Salary range: 80,000$ - 120,000$ per year (depending on experience and location)

Reports to: Lead Infrastructure Engineer

Key responsibilities

  • Design and implement cloud architectures(Iaas/Paas) across multiple providers
  • Develop Infrastructure-as-code: author, review and maintain Terraform/CloudFormation modules.
  • Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines to automate deployment and testing.
  • Collaborate with development teams to enable secure, reliable platform services

Required qualifications, skills & attributes

Essential
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent experience.
  • 3+ years professional experience operating cloud infrastructure(AWS, Azure or GCP).
  • Proficient with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform or CloudFormation).
  • Experience with at least one scripting language (Python, Bash) and Git-based workflows.
  • Familiarity with container platforms (Docker, Kubernetes) and logging/monitoring tooling.
Preferable
  • Security certifications such as CISSP, OSCP, CEH or cloud security certs (CCSP/AWS Security).
  • Experience integrating security tooling with CI/CD and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP).
  • Background in secure architecture reviews and compliance frameworks (ISO27001, PCI, SOC2).

Specific tasks

  1. Investigate and triage triggered alerts; document findings and remediation steps.
  2. Run monthly vulnerability scans and track remediation with engineering teams.
  3. Create and maintain detection rules and automated response playbooks.

Candidate notes & benefits

Benefits: remote/hybrid working options, training allowance, paid certification support, flexible hours.

How to apply: attach CV + concise cover letter.

Quick tips:

  • Highlight projects that show automation and measurable outcomes.
  • Include links to code samples or detection rule examples where relevant.

Recruitment & selection process

Typical stages:

  1. Initial CV screening
  2. Technical take-home or coding exercise
  3. Technical interview (system design, troubleshooting)
  4. Final HR & cultural fit interview
